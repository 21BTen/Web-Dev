1.Kimchi recipe (Napa cabbage kimchi)

  • Prep Time: 3 hours
  • Brining: 6 hours
  • Total Time: 9 hours
  • Servings: 7 litre

INGREDIENTS

Ingredients Quantity
napa cabbage 4 kg
water 16 cups
cooking salt 1/2 cup
cancochi 3-4 tablespoons

Step 1: Cut the napa cabbage into quarters and rinse it in running water. (do not damage the stem).

Step 2: Rub the cabbage petals well with salt, put them in a large cup and press it down with a press. Leave the cabbage for 5-6 hours until the cabbage is well salted and becomes soft. Turn the cabbage over every 2 hours.

Step 3: After the cabbage has become well salted and has become soft, rinse it under running water from excess salt.

Step 4: Rub the cabbage with a special cankochisauce.

3.Pancakes

  • Total Time: 20 mins
  • Servings: 10-15 pancakes

INGREDIENTS

Ingredients Quantity
milk 0.5 l
egg 3
vegetable oil 1 tablespoon
flour 250 g
sugar 1 tablespoon
salt 1 pinch

Step 1:Mix eggs, salt, sugar and whisk them, gradually introduce flour. Pour in the milk and stir until smooth.

Step 2:Add vegetable oil and leave the dough for 10-15 minutes.

Step 3:Heat a frying pan with vegetable oil and fry the dough on both sides.
